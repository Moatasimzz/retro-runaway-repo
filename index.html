RUNAWAY

Retro Runaway is a thrilling maze-based game where you navigate a car through tile paths. Your mission is to collect a key that unlocks the door to the next level. Along the way, gather as many coins as possible to boost your score! But watch outâ€”the mazes get trickier with each stage.

Can you find the key, grab the coins, and speed your way to victory?

HOW TO PLAY THE MAZE GAME

Navigate through each level's maze, collect all coins before time runs out, and progress to the next challenge!

Objective

Collect all the coins in the maze to unlock the next level. Time is limited, so act quickly!

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move through the maze. Navigate carefully to avoid losing precious seconds.

Levels

Each level is progressively harder, with more complex maze patterns and faster timers.

Tips

Plan your path! Take note of where the coins are located before you start moving.
