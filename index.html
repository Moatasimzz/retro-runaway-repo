HOW TO PLAY THE MAZE GAME

Navigate through each level's maze, collect the key and raise your score by collecting coins before time runs out, and progress to the next challenge!

Objective

Collect the key to unlock the next level. Don't forget your score and collect the coins. Time is limited, so act quickly!

Controls

Use the arrow keys to move through the maze. Navigate carefully to avoid losing precious seconds.

Levels

Each level is progressively harder, with more complex maze patterns and faster timers.

Tips

Plan your path! Take note of where the coins are located before you start moving.
